SIGIR'99: Music Information Retrieval


Limitations


  • music-notation related information (plus reference information)

  • limited to copyright free sources

  • limited to classical and traditional (i.e. folk) materials

  • emphasis on 17th-19th century repertories

  • limited to handful of data formats

    • MIDI (sound-related applications)

    • MuseData (printing & general applications)

    • Humdrum (analytic applications)
