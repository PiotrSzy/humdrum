SIGIR'99: Music Information Retrieval


The New(?) Reality


LIBRARIANS
  • Because librarians have access to information and have an informed understanding of materials, they are very well placed to do original research.

  • Librarians often produce reference tools that amount to "primary sources".
RESEARCHERS
  • Are engaged in acquisitions (usually "personal collections").

  • With the Web: citations are becoming direct links (overlapping with the traditional task of reference librarians).
